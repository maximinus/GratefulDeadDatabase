About the GDD

GDD is yet another attempt to create a database of Grateful Dead shows and sets. The data was taken from a number of sources; primarily the tapes themselves, but I also cross-checked against Deadbase, https://www.setlists.net/, https://jerrybase.com/, https://github.com/jefmsmit/gdshowsdb. Timings were data-mined from archive.org. All data here is public domain, and the complete database itself can be found on the "downloads" tab.

What's Missing
  • Shows with no setlists are currently not in the database.
  • Some timings are missing.
  • The segues are mostly missing.
What's To Come
  • The timings will be completed.
  • The segue information will be added.
  • Beats Per Minute (BPM) as a running average will be added.
  • List of musical guests will be added.
  • Maps of all tours.
  • A page allowing you to search for shows with extreme fine-tuning.
  • The weather data will include wind speed, snow, sunrise and sunset times.
